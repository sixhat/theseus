New macs are rubish

I just want to say something about new macs. They are rubish. I never had freezes and reboots with old macs.

I've been running a M2 air for more than one year and it has frozen and rebooted on me several times. If I push it too hard it panics and reboots.

Early on it was Wifi connection that wasn't stable and forced me to disable "Continuity" with my iPad, to try to keep it running longer than 5 minutes.

Now with AI trying to run the smallest Ollama models is a challenge. It might just poop on you and reboot. Twice in the last hour. ME NOT HAPPY.

Sorry, just wanted to get this out of my chest
